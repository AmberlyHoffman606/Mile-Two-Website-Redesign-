BENEFITS SUMMARY

Doing great work is at the heart of what we do, and we feel strongly that our team members are at their best when they work in an environment that supports their professional AND personal needs. In addition to providing ample PTO, we offer a highly competitive benefits package, the majority of which is at zero cost to our employees. Despite being a smaller company, we strive to offer our team all the benefits of working for a larger organization. This comprehensive package includes medical, dental, vision, life and disability insurance, as well as, a 401k plan with a company match. Our flexible and casual work environment encourages our employees to have a positive work-life balance while doing interesting work in a dynamic organizational climate.

JOIN THE FUN

We are a growing team of energetic problem-solvers and technologists. Located in the innovation district in Dayton, Ohio, our workplace encourages discussion and divergent thinking. We foster vibrant connections between our team, our community, and our clients. Stop by to meet our team and learn more about our work.
