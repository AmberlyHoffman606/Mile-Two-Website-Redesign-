MANIFESTO

You create the dream - then you bring it into your world. You better get your coat out, this is going to be a cold painting. This is the time to get out all your flustrations, much better than kicking the dog around the house or taking it out on your spouse. Don't kill all your dark areas - you need them to show the light. You can create beautiful things - but you have to see them in your mind first. You have freedom here. The only guide is your heart.
